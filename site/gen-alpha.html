design philosophy

The Gamified Universe. Everything is interactive, rewarding, and playful. They don't distinguish between apps and games - they expect dopamine-optimized, accessibility-first experiences where browsing feels like play.

🌟 The Three Pillars

  • Gamification Native - XP bars, achievements, streaks, collectibles. Progress must be visible and celebrated.
  • Radical Accessibility - WCAG AAA is the floor, not the ceiling. Neurodiversity-friendly patterns are mandatory.
  • Haptic-Audio-Visual Harmony - Sounds, vibrations, and visuals work together. Full sensory design.
