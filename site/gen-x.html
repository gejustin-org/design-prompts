Philosophy

The Slacker Era Aesthetic

Whatever, Man — Authentic Apathy Meets DIY Rebellion

This is the visual language of the generation that grew up with MTV, watched the Berlin Wall fall, and came of age in the shadow of Boomer idealism. Gen X design captures the cynical independence, grunge authenticity, and early digital frontier of 1985-1999.

It's zines, mixtapes, dial-up modems, and the belief that trying too hard is the ultimate sin.
