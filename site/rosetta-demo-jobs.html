About this role

We're looking for a Software Engineer to join the Core Product team at Handshake. You'll build features that connect students with employers, helping millions of early-talent job seekers launch their careers.

You'll work across the full stack with a focus on frontend, collaborating closely with product designers and data scientists to ship experiments and iterate quickly. Our stack is React, TypeScript, and GraphQL on the frontend with Ruby on Rails powering the API layer.

This role is ideal for engineers who care deeply about user experience and want their work to have a tangible impact on the career outcomes of the next generation.

Requirements

  • • 2+ years of professional experience building web applications with React and TypeScript
  • • Strong understanding of modern frontend patterns, state management, and component architecture
  • • Experience with GraphQL or RESTful APIs and comfort working across the stack when needed
  • • Track record of shipping user-facing features and iterating based on data and feedback
  • • BS/MS in Computer Science or equivalent practical experience

Benefits

  • • Competitive equity package and 401(k) with company match
  • • Comprehensive medical, dental, and vision coverage for you and your dependents
  • • Flexible PTO, 16 weeks parental leave, and monthly wellness stipend
