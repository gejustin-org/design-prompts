Overview

Rosetta is Handshake's design system — a shared language for building interfaces that feel professional, approachable, and trustworthy. Every token, component, and pattern serves this intent.

The Vibe

Clean. Approachable. Trustworthy. Modern.

If this design were a space

A modern coworking space with clean desks and good lighting. White walls with occasional accent plants. Natural light through large windows. Professional but not stuffy — people wear nice casual. Quiet enough to focus, social enough to feel human.

If this design were music

Lo-fi beats or ambient electronic. Professional podcast intro music. Nothing distracting, nothing boring. The kind of background that helps you focus on the work.

What This Design Is NOT

Dark or moody Playful or whimsical Trendy or experimental Corporate or sterile Loud or attention-seeking

THE BOLD FACTOR

Signature elements that make Rosetta unmistakably Rosetta.

01

Dark Teal, Not Blue

#04191B is the hero color. Sophisticated, distinctive. Avoids the "generic SaaS blue" trap.

02

Noi Grotesk + Alternates

ss03 and ss11 features give subtle character. Geometric precision with humanist warmth.

03

Shadow-First Elevation

Soft shadows over heavy borders. Cards float rather than sit. Depth without visual noise.

04

4px Grid Discipline

Every measurement snaps to the 4px grid. Subconscious harmony across the entire system.

05

Status Dual Variants

Each status has both a light background tint and saturated foreground. Flexible, consistent patterns.

06

Sansplomb for Impact

Bold condensed energy reserved for hero headlines and marketing. Never in product UI.

07

White Space as Feature

Generous padding and margins. The interface breathes. Content has room to be comprehended.

08

Semantic Color Restraint

Color means something. Blue = info. Green = success. Red = error. Never decorative.
